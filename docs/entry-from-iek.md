# Entry from Insight & Evolution Kit (IEK)

## Boundary Briefing

You are entering the Strategic Direction Kit from the Insight & Evolution Kit (Layer 7). This document describes what you bring, what this kit expects, and what happens next.

---

## What You Bring

- A frozen Evolution Signal (ES) with a `re-discover` signal in §6
- The ES §6 rationale explaining why strategic re-evaluation is warranted
- The ES §7 recommended actions (Discovery-type actions inform the new SBR)

## What This Kit Expects

The ES `re-discover` signal indicates that a service or capability warrants **strategic re-evaluation** — not merely a product refinement. This kit creates a new Strategic Bet Record (SBR) that captures the re-evaluated strategic direction.

**Decision point:** A human product owner must decide whether the re-discover signal warrants a new SBR (new strategic bet) or should route directly to PIK (refinement within an existing bet's scope). This decision happens before entering SDK.

- **New strategic question** → Enter SDK. Create a new SBR citing the ES artifact ID in §7 Strategic Context.
- **Refinement within existing bet** → Route to PIK directly. The existing SBR and PPR remain unchanged.

## What Happens Here

If entering SDK:

1. An executive sponsor authors a new SBR informed by the ES findings
2. The SBR is validated and frozen
3. The next PPR cycle includes the new SBR in the ranking
4. If the new SBR is ranked above the cut line, it routes to PIK for discovery
